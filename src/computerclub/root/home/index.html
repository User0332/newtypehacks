Our Programs

CyberPatriot

AFA CyberPatriot is a nation-wide cybersecurity competition run by the Air Force Association. Participants from all over the US are eligible, and our club has multiple CyberPatriot teams. Join today to become a part of one!

Robotics

FTC (First Tech Challenge) Robotics is run by the company FIRST Inspires. In the First Tech Challenge, high school students design, build, program, and compete with robots that play a game, outlined each year/season. Top teams qualify for the State and World Championships. Robotics is a great way to foster mechanical, software, and interpersonal skills. Our club has one FTC Robotics team. We're always looking for new members so join the computer club today and be a part of our team!

Project Programming

If you are interested in programming, the project programming branch of our club is right for you! Each meeting we teach new technologies and build projects using those technologies. Programming Club is a great way to network with other students that are into coding, learn new concepts for programming different applications, and work on larger-scale projects with other students.

Competitive Programming

If you like the sound of our programming branch but you want to take your skills or expertise to the next level, our Competitive Programming Program is for you. We gather students from our Programming Club to participate in various competitive computing competitions such as ASCL (American Computer Science League), CTFs, and more!
